Recipe of Jalebi

Ingredients

    For The Batter

  • 1/2 tsp dry yeast
  • 1/2 cup plain flour (maida)

    • For The Sugar Syrup

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp saffron (kesar) strands strands
  • 2 pinches saffron colour

    • Other ingredients

  • Ghee for deep-frying

    • For The Garnish

  • 1/2 tsp cardamom (elaichi) powder
  • 1 tsp almond (badam) slivers
  • 1 tsp pistachio slivers

Procedure

    For The Batter

  • Combine the yeast with 5 tbsp of warm water in a bowl and keep aside for 5 to 7 minutes.
  • Combine this yeast mixture with plain flour in a bowl and mix well.
  • Add 2 tbsp of water if required to make a thick batter and cover and keep aside for 10 to 15 minutes.

    • For the sugar syrup

  • Add the sugar to the boiling milk, mix well and cook on a medium flame for 1 minute.Combine the sugar and 1 cup of water in a broad non-stick pan, mix well and cook on a high flame for 12 minutes or till the sugar syrup is of 1 thread consistency.
  • Combine the saffron strands, saffron colour and 2 tsp of sugar syrup in a bowl and add to the sugar syrup and mix well. Keep aside.

    • How to proceed

  • Select a plastic bottle with a small hole in the lid and fill with the batter.
  • Heat the ghee in a broad non-stick pan and press the bottle to form a coil shaped jalebi, starting from the inside to the outside.
  • Deep-fry till the jalebis turn golden brown in colour from both the sides. Remove and wait for 1 minute.
