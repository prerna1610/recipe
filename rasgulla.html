Recipie of Rasgulla

Ingredients

  • 5 cups of cows milk
  • 2 1/2 cups of buffalo's milk
  • 1 1/2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 cup Sugar
  • 5 cups of Water

Procedure

  • Combine the cow's milk and buffalo's milk in a broad and deep non-stick pan and bring it to boil. NOTE: If buffalo's milk is not available then you can use 5 cups of cow milk instead of 2 1/2 cups.
  • Switch off of the flame and wait for 1 minute, while stirring occasionally.
  • Add the lemon juice gradually and keep stirring gently.
  • Allow it to stand for 1/2 minute to curdle. It is completely curdled, when the chenna and the whey(the greenish water) separate out.
  • Strain using a muslin cloth. Discard the whey.
  • Place the muslin cloth with the Chenna in the Bowl of fresh water and wash it for atleast 2 to 3 times.
  • Tie and hang for 30 minutes for the extra waater to drain out.
  • Now, put 5 cups of water in a steamer or a pressure cooker and add the sugar and bring it to a boil, while stirring occasionally so that the suggar dissolves completely.
  • Meanwhile, squeeze the muslin cloth to drain any more water remaining.
  • Place the muslin cloth on a flat pplate, open it and knead the chenna very well using your plams for 3 to 4 minutes or till the chenna is smooth and free of lumps.
  • Divide the chenna into 16 equal portions and roll each portion into small balls between your palms.
  • Put the chenna balls into the sugar water and cover and steam for 7 to 8 minutes.
  • Switch off the flame and allow it to stand in the steamer for 10 to 15 minutes.
  • Remove gently into a bowl,refrigerate and serve chilled.
