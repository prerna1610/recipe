Recipie of petha

Ingredients

  • Petha Fruit - 2 kg
  • Sugar - 5 cup ( 1kg 200 gms)
  • Limestone - 2 tsp or 1 tsp Fitkari
  • 2 tbsp almonds / badam (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp pistachios (chopped)
  • ▢¼ tsp saffron / kesar
  • ¼ tsp cardamom powder / elachi powder

Procedure

  • Take a ripe petha fruit, remove the soft pulpy part, peel the green skin, cut all it into smaller pieces. Poka ll pieces with fork.
  • Take 2 litre water in a vessel, add limestone to the water and stir it.Put the Petha pieces in limestone water. Keep it for 10 to 12 hours.
  • Then drain the limestone water, wash off the petha pieces well. Keep in a plate.
  • Pour 2 litre water in vessel to boil the pethas. Add pethas. Boil them on high flame for 20 minutes.
  • Petha is boiled well and ready, take out the pethas from water and keep in strainer,then keep in plate.
  • Take some sugar, add water to it, cook till sugar dissolves. Stir at regular intervals. Add the petha pices to sugar syrup, cook until dense on high flame.
  • When syrup is of desired consistency then, Pick it up and keep to cool. Let pieces stay in syrup for 6 to 7 hours or overnight.
  • When dense, lift and keep the petha pieces over the net. Arrange all and keep under the fan to dry for few hours.
  • Agra's famous Petha sweet is ready. Fill petha in a container, keep outside fridge and relish it for upto 2 months.
