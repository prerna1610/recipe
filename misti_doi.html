Recipe of Misti Doi

Ingredients

  • 1 ltr milk
  • 1/4 cup milk powder
  • 1/4 cup sugar caramelized
  • 2 tbsp plain yogurt
  • 1 tbsp chopped pistachio and almonds

Procedure

  • In a deep heavy bottom pan and mix milk powder and bring it to boil.
  • In another pan add sugar and melt on medium high flame till sugar gets completely melt and turns nice in golden colour.or you can say cook,until it's caramelized.keep on stirring continuously while cooking.
  • Now pour in the boiling milk mixture.now cook on medium flame till it reduce to 1/2 of it's original quantity by stirring occasionally.check for the sweetness of requires add more as per your taste.once done,take off from the heat and let it cool down for some time.
  • Take yogurt into milk and whisk thoroughly until seeing the bubbles.pour the mixture into a pot.
  • Now cover it and keep in warm place for few hours.or may be overnight to set,refrigerate,and serve the chilled mishti doi.garnish with pistachios and almonds.
