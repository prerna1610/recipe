Recipe of Doodh Pak

Ingredients

  • Milk 4-5 cups
  • Basmati Rice soaked 3 1/2 tablespoons
  • Desi ghee 1/2 tablespoon
  • Green cardamom powder 1/2 teaspoon
  • Sugar 7 tablespoons
  • Charoli/chironji 1 teaspoon
  • Pistachios slivered 8-10
  • Almonds slivered 8-10

Procedure

  • Heat ghee in non stick pan. Bring milk to boil in deep non stick pan and simmer for 10-12 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  • Add rice to ghee and saut√© till light brown. Add milk and mix. Simmer on low heat for 20-25 minutes.Add cardamom powder, mix well and cook till rice is done.
  • Add sugar and mix well. Add charoli and mix and cook for 10-12 minutes more.Garnish with pistachio and almond slivers. Serve warm or chilled.
