Recipe of Kheer

Ingredients

  • 1 ltr milk)
  • 1 handful of Rice
  • 5tbsp Sugar
  • 1/2 ltr milk
  • raisins
  • soaked almonds
  • crushed cardamom

Procedure

  • In a pan boil milk and add a handful of soaked rice and keep stirring.
  • Add sugar to the kheer and keep stirring continuously.
  • when it becomes thick add more milk to it.
  • Keep stirring the kheer and add raisins, soaked almonds, crushed cardamom Rice Kheer is ready to be served!
